### Raphael Sousa Santos

Composer and programmer  

Raphael Sousa Santos is a composer, visual artist and programmer based in the Netherlands. He experiments with the development of programming languages and the use of random processes, evolutionary algorithms and spectral techniques for the creation and performance of music and visual arts. His research interest spans audio analysis and synthesis, music theory and computer assisted composition with a focus on the role of abstraction, analogy, syntactic manipulation and the use of language during the compositional or analytical process. Besides developing software for audio and music, Raphael Sousa Santos also has experience developing distributed systems handling high amounts of traffic and data.

#### Programming synthesizers live in OCaml

In this talk we describe two OCaml libraries: one for defining communicating processes and modifying their code live without interruption and a second one for describing and playing audio synthesizers. We demonstrate and discuss how these can provide the foundation for live coding performances with a statically typed functional programming language.
